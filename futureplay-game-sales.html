Case Study

GameSales Insights: Forecasting Success

Leveraging historical sales data and advanced analytics to forecast future game sales, supporting strategic marketing and product decisions.

Role: Data Analyst Timeline: Mar–Apr 2025 Methods: Regression · ML · Visualization
Jump to Solution See Research

The Problem

Game publishers lacked reliable sales forecasts, leading to suboptimal marketing spend and product launches. Fragmented data and underutilized historical trends limited strategic planning.

“How can we use our data to predict future sales and optimize our strategy?”
High impact Advanced analytics

Challenge: Aggregating, cleaning, and modeling large datasets for actionable predictions.

Objective

  • Forecast future game sales with high accuracy
  • Visualize predictions and key drivers for stakeholders
  • Deliver actionable recommendations for marketing and product teams

Solution: Predictive Sales Dashboard

Built a predictive dashboard using regression and ML models. Visualized forecasts and key drivers for stakeholders. Provided recommendations for marketing and product teams.

  • Aggregate and clean sales data

Results & Learnings

Stakeholder feedback confirmed:

  • Forecasts supported marketing and product decisions
  • Stakeholders gained confidence in data-driven planning
  • Resource allocation and campaign targeting improved
  • Early feedback and iterative design were critical
Stakeholder Feedback
  • ✔ Dashboard usability rated highly by users.
  • ✔ Visualizations provided strong storytelling value.
  • ✔ Actionable recommendations were implemented in campaigns.
Key Learnings
  • ★ Early stakeholder feedback is critical for analytics projects.
  • ★ Prioritization frameworks help clarify MVP scope.
  • ★ Future iterations could add real-time prediction APIs and campaign optimization analytics.
