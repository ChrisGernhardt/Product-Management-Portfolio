FuturePlay Game Sales Case Study
Overview

Leveraging historical sales data and advanced analytics to forecast future game sales, supporting strategic marketing and product decisions. This project combined regression, ML, and dashboard visualizations to deliver actionable insights for stakeholders.

  • 🧑‍💻 Role: Data Analyst
  • 📅 Timeline: Mar–Apr 2025
  • 🛠️ Methods: Regression · ML · Visualization
Jump to Solution See Research
Problem Challenge
Forecasting Challenges

Game publishers lacked reliable sales forecasts, leading to suboptimal marketing spend and product launches. Fragmented data and underutilized historical trends limited strategic planning. The absence of a unified dashboard and actionable insights made it difficult to optimize strategy and resource allocation.

  • 🔎 Identify gaps in historical sales and marketing data
  • 📝 Survey teams and stakeholders to uncover forecasting needs
  • 💡 Deliver actionable recommendations and predictive dashboards
Objective Goal
PRD & Research Plan

The Product Requirements Document (PRD) defined the scope and success metrics for a predictive dashboard. The research plan included stakeholder interviews, surveys, and analysis of historical data to clarify requirements and validate modeling approaches. The goal was to enable data-driven sales forecasting and strategic decision-making for marketing and product teams.
