Type: SaaS Product Management
Problem: Product teams often struggle to connect feature delivery with real business impact, leading to wasted effort and missed opportunities.
Objective: Align product strategy with business goals to maximize value, retention, and growth.
Approach: Conducted stakeholder interviews, analyzed user data, prioritized initiatives using MoSCoW and impact/effort scoring, defined MVPs, and planned launches with clear success metrics.
Outcome: Delivered features that improved retention, reduced churn, and increased NPS. Enabled cross-functional teams to focus on outcomes, not just outputs.

Key Impact Areas

  • Retention: Improved onboarding and feature adoption led to higher user retention rates.
  • Churn Reduction: Addressed top pain points, reducing churn among high-value segments.
  • Revenue Growth: Launched upsell features and improved conversion flows.
  • Stakeholder Alignment: Created shared understanding of goals and priorities.
  • User Satisfaction: Increased NPS and positive feedback through targeted improvements.
