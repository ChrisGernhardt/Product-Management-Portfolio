About Me
I'm a SaaS professional turned Product Manager, passionate about building products that solve real user problems and drive measurable business outcomes. Based in Berlin, I bring 10+ years of experience at the intersection of operations, strategy, and data, now fully dedicated to creating products that users love.

What sets me apart is my ability to combine data insights with product strategy: I translate complex analysis into actionable decisions, align stakeholders around a clear vision, and deliver with agile execution. My background in SaaS gives me a deep understanding of how to scale products and balance user needs with business goals.

I believe in clear communication, empathy, and evidence-based decision-making — creating products that not only serve users but also accelerate growth.
Why Product?
After years in SaaS, I realized my favorite part of the work wasn’t just running operations — it was shaping what we built and why. Transitioning into product management has allowed me to do exactly that: connect strategy, design, and technology to turn ideas into real solutions. My past roles gave me the analytical rigor and stakeholder management skills; product gives me the canvas to apply them with impact.
