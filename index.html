Featured Case Study

The MakeYourMap (MYM) project focused on improving navigation accuracy by detecting and flagging duplicate or similar street names during address search. In dense urban environments, users often navigate to the wrong location because of ambiguous addresses, causing delays, frustration, and reduced trust in the platform. The goal was to design a feature that would reduce navigation errors by at least 30% and ensure ≥90% correct address selection when flags were shown.
