Case Study

Influence Staffing Project

Analyzing staffing patterns and their impact on project outcomes using survey data, aggregation, and visualization.

Role: Data Analyst Timeline: May–Jun 2025 Methods: Survey · Aggregation · Visualization
Jump to Solution See Research

The Problem

Teams struggled to identify optimal staffing levels, leading to inefficiencies and missed project goals. Complex data made it difficult to visualize and act on insights.

“We need clear data to guide our staffing decisions and improve project outcomes.”
High impact Medium effort MVP

Challenge: Aggregating and visualizing team data for actionable insights.

Objective

  • Identify optimal staffing levels for project success.
  • Visualize complex team data for stakeholders.
  • Deliver actionable recommendations.

Research & Approach

Data Sources

  • Historical staffing and project delivery data
  • Team surveys (n=12)
  • Stakeholder interviews

Methods

  • Data cleaning and aggregation
  • Descriptive statistics and visualization
  • Dashboard creation

Key Steps

  1. Reviewed historical data and identified needs
  2. Planned and acquired relevant data
  3. Integrated and validated data sources
  4. Prepared and aggregated data for analysis
  5. Uncovered patterns and validated assumptions
  6. Delivered actionable insights and dashboards

Solution: Staffing Insights Dashboard

Built a dashboard to aggregate and visualize staffing data, enabling teams to make informed decisions. Provided recommendations based on key metrics and trends.

  • Aggregate staffing and project data
  • Visualize key metrics for stakeholders
  • Recommend optimal staffing levels
Effort
~80 hrs
Target accuracy
≥90%

Challenge: Ensuring dashboard usability and actionable recommendations for all teams.

Dashboard Visuals

Results & Learnings

Stakeholder feedback confirmed:

  • Stakeholders gained confidence in data-driven staffing decisions
  • Improved project outcomes and team efficiency
  • Clear dashboards and recommendations enabled actionable change
Stakeholder Feedback
  • ✔ Dashboard usability rated highly by users.
  • ✔ Visualizations provided strong storytelling value.
  • ✔ Actionable recommendations were implemented by teams.
Key Learnings
  • ★ Early stakeholder feedback is critical for analytics projects.
  • ★ Prioritization frameworks help clarify MVP scope.
  • ★ Future iterations could add predictive staffing models and advanced analytics.
