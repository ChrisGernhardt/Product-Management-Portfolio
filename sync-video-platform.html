Sync Video Platform: Collaborative Video for Remote Teams

Enabling remote teams to share, annotate, and discuss video content in real time for improved engagement and communication.

Role: Product Manager Timeline: Mar–Apr 2025 Methods: Stakeholder Interviews · Wireframes · Prototypes · Agile
Jump to Solution See Research

The Problem

Remote teams struggle to collaborate effectively on video content due to fragmented tools and lack of real-time feedback. This leads to communication gaps, slower review cycles, and reduced engagement.

“We need a way to review and discuss videos together, even when we’re not in the same room.”
High impact Medium effort MVP

Challenge: Integrating seamless video annotation and feedback while supporting distributed workflows.

Objective

  • Enable real-time video sharing and annotation.
  • Improve team engagement and feedback cycles.
  • Deliver a usable MVP within two sprints.

Research & Insights

Stakeholder interviews and user surveys revealed key pain points in remote video collaboration. Teams wanted integrated annotation, easy sharing, and real-time discussion features.

Methods

  • Stakeholder interviews (n=10), remote-first teams
  • User surveys and feedback sessions
  • Wireframe and prototype testing

Key Findings

  • Teams lacked a unified tool for video review and discussion
  • Annotation and timestamped comments were highly requested
  • Integration with Slack and Google Drive was important

Solution: Sync Video Platform MVP

The MVP focused on core features: video upload, sharing, annotation, and real-time comments. Advanced integrations and analytics were scoped for future releases.

Planned OKRs for Post-Launch Evaluation

Objective 1
Increase team engagement
  • KR1 Achieve +25% engagement rate in first quarter.
  • KR2 Reach ≥80% feature adoption among target teams.
  • KR3 Collect at least 30 positive feedback entries on collaboration features.
Objective 2
Reduce review cycles
  • KR1 Achieve 30% reduction in review time for video content.
  • KR2 Maintain ≥70% retention of collaboration features after 3 months.
  • KR3 Generate at least 20 positive user feedback entries mentioning annotation and discussion features.
Objective 3
Improve team satisfaction
  • KR1 Raise relevant NPS score by at least +8 points among platform users.
  • KR2 Increase task success rate in video review flows to 90% in usability tests.
  • KR3 Achieve ≥4.3/5 average rating on post-collaboration surveys for platform users.

Results & Learnings

Stakeholder feedback confirmed:

  • The problem was well-researched and clearly defined.
  • The feature design balanced impact vs. effort effectively.
  • Wireframes and prototypes provided strong storytelling value.
