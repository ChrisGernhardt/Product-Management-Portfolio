Influencer Staffing Project

The Influencer Staffing Project analyzes staffing patterns and their impact on project outcomes. By integrating historical data, stakeholder surveys, and advanced analytics, the project delivers actionable insights and dashboards to support data-driven staffing decisions and continuous improvement.

Problem Challenge
Staffing Data Gaps

Staffing decisions were often made without a unified view of historical project delivery and team capacity. The lack of integrated data and actionable insights led to inefficiencies and missed opportunities for optimization.

  • 🔎 Identify gaps in historical staffing and delivery data
  • 📝 Survey teams and stakeholders to uncover data needs
  • 💡 Deliver actionable recommendations and dashboards
Objective Goal
PRD & Research Plan

The Product Requirements Document (PRD) outlined the need for a unified dashboard to aggregate staffing and delivery data. The research plan included stakeholder interviews and surveys to clarify requirements, validate assumptions, and ensure the solution addressed real-world challenges.

  • 📄 PRD: Defined scope, MVP, and success metrics for the dashboard
  • 🗣️ Research Plan: Gathered qualitative and quantitative insights from teams
  • 🎯 Objective: Enable data-driven staffing decisions and continuous improvement

  2. 🧭 Identified data needs and gaps (2.2 Identifying Data Needs)
  3. 📦 Planned and acquired relevant data sources (2.3 Planning Data-Driven Initiative, 2.4 Acquiring Relevant Data)

  5. 📊 Uncovered patterns, validated assumptions, and visualized insights
  6. 💡 Delivered actionable dashboards and recommendations (2.10 Interim Report)
Solution Dashboard
Staffing Insights Dashboard

Developed a dashboard to aggregate, visualize, and analyze influenza staffing data. The dashboard enabled teams to identify gaps, track key metrics, and make informed decisions. Recommendations were based on historical trends, survey results, and stakeholder feedback.

  • 📊 Aggregate staffing and project data
  • 📈 Visualize key metrics for stakeholders
  • 💡 Recommend optimal staffing levels
Effort
~80 hrs
Target accuracy
≥90%

Challenge: Ensuring dashboard usability and actionable recommendations for all teams.

Feedback

Learnings
